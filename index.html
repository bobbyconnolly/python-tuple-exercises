Tuple Exercises

Important Note: In the following three exercises, tuples can be substituted with lists without changing the behavior of the code. However, this is not always true in general Python programming.

For these examples, you can think of tuples as immutable lists. But remember, there are cases where tuples provide special behavior that lists cannot replicate. Exercise 4 will demonstrate such a case.
